About JAIPK

JAIPK is dedicated to preserving and providing access to a wide range of organizational content. Our web archive repository ensures that all stored content is easily accessible to authorized users via the internet.

Vission

To be an efficient and superior Islamic affairs management institution in Malaysia by 2025.

Mission

Strengthening Islamic Affairs Services with Maqasid Syariah Care through Education, Culture, Control in Perak to create a Khaira Ummah Community

Moto

Merintis Kegemilangan Ummah.

Objective

1. Strengthen organizational capabilities and capacities to improve the efficiency of the delivery system.

2. Empowering the Islamic da'wah agenda in a syumul manner towards the formation of khaira ummah based on the Quran and As-Sunnah.

3. Empowering the administration and enforcement of Islamic law to sovereign the implementation of Islamic law.

4. Strengthen the management of Islamic affairs through research and development to improve the quality of life of the prosperous ummah.

5. Strengthening strategic partnerships with agencies.
